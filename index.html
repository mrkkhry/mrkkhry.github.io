Mark Khoury Design is the personal practice of Toronto based industrial designer Mark Khoury. His work - consisting of objects, furniture, and spaces - reflects his focus on iterative design ideation, material exploration, and the leveraging of fabrication knowledge to realize tangible design solutions. He is inspired by the joys of the world around him and values timeless style and construction, yielding work intended to be used, worn down, and loved for years to come.
